About me

I am an Assistant Professor of Computational Linguistic at Université de Paris in the Linguistic Departement. Before that, I was Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Departement of Université Paris Saclay and member of LIMSI.

My research interests lie in the application of machine learning to study language.
